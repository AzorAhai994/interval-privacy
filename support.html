Common questions

How do I preview and export my video?
After capturing frames, tap the thumbnail in the bottom-left corner to open the preview screen. From there you can play back your frames, adjust timing, and export or share your video.

What does Interval Pro unlock?
Interval Pro is a one-time purchase that unlocks 4K export, manual frame rate control (1–30 FPS), and removes the watermark. If you've purchased Pro on another device, use “Restore Purchases” to unlock it.

Where are my videos saved?
Exported videos are saved to your photo library when you choose to save them. Everything is processed locally on your device.
