Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Interval is an offline-first camera utility. Your photos and videos stay on your device. We respect your privacy by default and collect only minimal, anonymous usage data to improve the app.

Your Media

All photos and videos are captured, processed, and stored locally on your device. Interval never uploads, shares, or has access to your media. Exported videos are saved to your photo library only when you choose to save them.

Camera and Photo Library Access

Interval requires access to your device camera to capture frames. Access to the photo library is used only to save exported videos when you choose to do so.

Use of TelemetryDeck to analyze app usage

We use the privacy-friendly analytics service TelemetryDeck (provider: TelemetryDeck GmbH, Von-der-Tann-Str. 54, 86159 Augsburg, Germany) to analyze usage data. The use is based on Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR, as we require reliable and efficient tools for collecting app usage data in order to fulfill the contract with you, our customer.

What data is transferred?

The data processed by TelemetryDeck is completely anonymized and does not allow any conclusions to be drawn about personal information. The following data is collected, among other things:

  • an anonymized, untraceable user ID (per app installation),
  • actions defined by the app publisher (e.g., "app launched," "settings opened"),
  • a rounded timestamp (to the nearest hour),
  • device metadata (e.g., system version, app version, device type),
  • additional metadata defined by the app publisher (e.g., "number of items in the database").

What is not stored?

  • No IP addresses (not in logs, not in the database),
  • No cookies or tracking technologies,
  • no persistent identifiers that could be traced back to individuals.

The source code of the TelemetryDeck SDK is completely open source and available on GitHub: https://github.com/TelemetryDeck. Further information on the exact data processing by TelemetryDeck can be found at telemetrydeck.com/privacy and at telemetrydeck.com/docs/guides/privacy-faq/.

Purchases

Interval Pro is a one-time in-app purchase processed entirely by Apple. Interval does not receive or store your payment information.

Data Sharing

Interval does not sell or share your data with third parties for their own purposes. We do not maintain user accounts.

Children's Privacy

Interval does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13.
