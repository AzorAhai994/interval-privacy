Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Overview

Interval is a simple, offline-first camera utility. Your privacy is respected by default.

Data Collection

Interval does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

  • No user accounts
  • No analytics
  • No tracking
  • No third-party SDKs
  • No cloud services

Camera and Photo Library Access

Interval requires access to the device camera in order to capture photos frame by frame. Access to the photo library is used only to save exported videos when you choose to do so.

All photos and videos are processed and stored locally on your device. Interval never uploads or shares your media.

Data Sharing

Interval does not share data with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

Interval does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

Any updates to this privacy policy will be published on this page.
